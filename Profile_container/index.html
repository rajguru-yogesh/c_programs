Steven Gerhardt

Managing Partner,
 CEO

Steven Gerhardt leads the company’s direction, business development and vision. He is an enterprise software architect with 25 years of experience in developing, selling and managing software product delivery.

Prior to Element Blue, Steven served as the co-founder and principal of Ixion for eleven years. During this time, Ixion became a leading provider of business and consumer portals, including web content manager solutions.

Steven’s background also includes work in product marketing, biotechnology, and ISO standards accreditation. He has a Bachelor’s degree from Baylor University and a Biotechnology Master’s degree from Johns Hopkins University.

Vincent Njoroge

Managing Director, Africa

Vincent is the Managing Director for the Africa Region of Element Blue. Vincent previously led the regional office of IBM Global Business Services, as Associate Partner and GBS Leader East Africa. He has more than 17 years of experience in business and IT consulting, with expertise in business strategy, business development, product management and software development. Vincent first joined IBM GBS as a Managing Consultant where he led teams to provide strategic collaboration solutions and specialized in building portal solutions for Fortune 100 companies in industries including financial services, manufacturing, and oil & gas services.

Prior to his return to Nairobi, He was Director of Client Services and Director of Video for AOL’s Advertising division. In this role he handled sales and client management as well as technical integration support and advising on strategies for monetization of video content. He has made frequent public appearances at industry conferences and at events worldwide.

Vincent holds a BA in Business Administration with a minor in Computer Science, and an MBA from Bowling Green State University.

Sanjay Jalagam

Managing Director, Middle East and India

Sanjay Jalagam has more than 20 years of experience working in information technology encompassing software development, deployment, sales and customer support. Sanjay has extensive experience working with global customers in helping build and maintain enterprise solutions. He is the country manager responsible for all sales and delivery operations in India. He also manages the India Development Center responsible for offshore delivery for all geo locations.

Sanjay has worked in various different roles in sales, service and management at Oracle and Moore Process Automation (division of Siemens). While at Oracle, he was a part of Global Swat Team for the Process Manufacturing Industry and then was a part of Oracle Energy practice. Sanjay holds a Master’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering.
